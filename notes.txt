Goal is to try to stop children from destroying the house or hurting others

Player actions
    WASD-Move
    Space-Carry child (only one at a time) and tend to them (clean diapers, bandaid, ect.)
    Mouse-Stare at child (slows down that child)

Child actions
    Randomly move
    Wander outside
    Topple bookshelves
    Turn on oven
    Open door
    Open drawers
    Poop
